About Junior Hub

Starting out as a Code Newbie, it isn't uncommon to feel out of your depth and overwhelmed, we all feel like this (and not even just at the start!) At Junior Hub, there's no such thing as a silly question. Here at Junior Hub we are an all inclusive community full of developers from Junior to Senior developers. As well as having a popular slack channel we also put on events on how to get into coding and how you can kick start your coding career, with more to come!

Our Community Values

Junior Hub is a nice, warm and welcoming atmosphere and we want to ensure that it stays this way, so please respect our community values.

Be Nice

Opinions are a bit like arseholes. Everyone has one. If you disagree with something, this is a space to support each other, so there is no need to be mean in your delivery. 'No that's not right' to 'Have you tried?'

Be Mindful

We are all here to help each other, however be mindful that sometimes people won't be able to help you there and then. (Work, life, etc!)

Be Understanding

Everyone started as a junior at one point and not everyone explains things in the same way. We need to be understanding and not get frustrated with our fellow devs, we're here to support each other!

Be Helpful

If someone has a query and you think you can help, don't be afraid to say! Juniors and seniors alike. We are all growing together.

Be Humble

Pro's and Con's of tech- there is always something new to learn. There's no one person who knows everything.

Be Appreciative

We all help each other here, no one is paid support. If someone has helped you fix something and whether it's worked or not, thank them for taking the time out of your day to offer help!
